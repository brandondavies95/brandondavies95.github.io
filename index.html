The Couple

After meeting at Western Wyoming Community College in Rock Springs and being together for 3 years, we have decided to tie the knot. I went to Western Wyoming to play basketball my freshman year and Cassidee was already playing at Western. She was entering her sophomore year on the volleybal team when we met. Cassidee's Brazilian roommate Carol was invited to my dorm room by another Brazilian roommate of mine, Felipe. Carol brought Cassidee with her to my room and there was this instant connection. It was my first time talking to her, but I had seen her at a dance earlier in the night. I didn't think much it because I had no idea who she was. That first conversation in my dorm room lead to several others and the rest was history.

Some of our engagement photos taken at Snow Canyon in St. George by Trina Thornley.

We will have been together for quite some time before getting married, three and a half years to be exact. During that time we have done so many incredible things together. It's easy to get caught up in our daily lives and not realize just how far we've come. We started off together in the dorm rooms of Western Wyoming Community College, and now we are living in St. George finishing up school. From being a great college athlete all the way to now being a nurse graduated from nursing school, seeing Cassidee accomplish so many great things has made me realize how special she is. The passion she has towards things and also her work ethic are truly admirable.

Cass and I are a TEAM. When I mess up she makes sure to call me out and I do the same for her. Over the years we've had to lean on each other to get through some hard times. I know that no matter what I'll be able to come home to her and have her by my side. I'm thankful I found someone that makes me want to be a better person each day. Love is wanting more for someone than they want for themselves. I'm a firm believer in that.

Love is forgiving time and time again, sometimes against better judgment. Love is what can drive us to do things we would never once consider.
